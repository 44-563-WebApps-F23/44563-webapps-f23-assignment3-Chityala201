Araku India

Araku Valley, located in the Eastern Ghats of India, is a great vacation spot that offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Located in the state of Andhra Pradesh, Araku is renowned for its lush green hills, coffee plantations, and tribal culture. You can take a fun train ride through tunnels and see the pretty countryside on the Araku Valley Railway. There are also lovely waterfalls like Katiki and Chaparai, and cool caves called Borra Caves with cool rock formations.
